Notes

Baseline grid = 4px

All sizes (font, line-height and margins) are all currently in px, for testing purposes only. Sizes will need to be converted to em's.

We have used single-direction margin declarations to easily define vertical rhythm (external link).

All typography has yet to be tested. Our user research plan will be available soon.
