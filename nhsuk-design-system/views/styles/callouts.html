Warning

  • Time critical
  • May have a significant health impact soon
  • Addresses a common and significant misconception or patient mistake

User need

As a user I need to identify and understand the important content on the page (even if I don't read all the content) so that I get the best outcome for my situation and don't endanger my health.

Example

Carry on taking this medicine until you've completed the course, even if you feel better. If you stop your treatment early, the infection could come back.
